Transportation is a top-5 driver of chronic absenteeism, which affects 26% of US students post-COVID. When a bus is late or a student misses it, no one knows until it's already a crisis. Parents call the school, schools call dispatch, dispatch calls the driver — all reactive, all too late.

The Solution

MagicBusRoute is a real-time iOS platform that connects every party in a single secure app — so a delayed bus triggers an alert before a parent even wonders where their child is.
